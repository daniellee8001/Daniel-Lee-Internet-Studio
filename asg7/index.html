Kyōjurō Rengoku for Leader of the Demon Slayer Corps

Kyojuro is an extremely skilled swordsman that is a tactical genius that serves as a long time member of the Demon Slayer Corps. The Corps' job is to protect the public in secrecy by slaying human-eating demons. Among the Corps, he stands as one of the Hashira, these are a group of high ranking individuals that are some of the strongest and most skilled swordsmen of the entire Corps. He is a young man with flame-like hair, he has a very charismatic personality that draws people by his side and support. He is also a very warm character that sympathizes and protects others' well-being.

Kyojuro would make for a powerful yet humbling leader. He is a man that commands great confidence in his skills and leadership skills. He is shown to lead dozens of dozens of men to battle, supporting them in both physical and emotional troubles. He stands at the front of the line for problems yet will always remember to take care of individual needs of others. He stands with others, holding strong feelings of protection to his students and followers. This extends to the general public that he protects on a daily basis.

Running Mate: Mitsuri Kanroji

Mitsuri is one of Rengoku's oldest students, and is a powerful swordswoman and is a skilled tactician. She does not command as much leadership as Rengoku, however, has a massive compassionate heart for others. This makes her a leader that represents the people very well. She sees the faults or falls of people and supports them emotionally and will lead in a way that is more oriented towards the actual social aspect of leadership, rather than sheer physical action like Rengoku. She is a very kind and cheerful individual that allows her positive energy to bleed onto others.

Campaign Platform: New Stronger Leadership

Rengoku would not necessarily takeover the previous leader by force. In this world, the prior-existing leaadership members would die from young age from a sickness that is passed down by a curse. This shows that though leaders may be successful, it is discouraging to have so many leaders die so young and are weak from sickness. To ensure a strong stable leader, Rengoku yearns to establish a stronger presense as top brass in the Demon Slayer Corps to envoke confidence in their fellow swordsmen and to ensure that the Corps stays strong with his high level and efficient training and tactical organization.
