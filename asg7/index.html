Kyōjurō Rengoku for High Leader

Kyojuro is an extremely skilled swordsman that serves as a long time member of the Demon Slayer Corps. The Corps' job is to protect the public in secrecy by slaying human-eating demons. Among the Corps, he stands as one of the Hashira, these are a group of high ranking individuals that are some of the strongest and most skilled swordsmen of the entire Corps. He is a young man with flame-like hair, he has a very charismatic personality that draws people by his side and support. He is also a very warm character that sympathizes and protects others' well-being.

Kyojuro would make for a powerful yet humbling leader. He is a man that commands great confidence in his skills and leadership skills. He is shown to lead dozens of dozens of men to battle, supporting them in both physical and emotional troubles. He stands at the front of the line for problems yet will always remember to take care of individual needs of others. He stands with others, holding strong feelings of protection to his students and followers. This extends to the general public that he protects on a daily basis.
