What is the difference between structured content and semantic HTML?

Structured content describes the way or methods any particular clust of data or information
is found by algorithms. To be a bit more specific, it is one of the first things that

is displayed, however, it makes more emphasis on the webpage itself on how it visually presents
itself to allow a meaningful relationship with the consumer who is viewing it. It can often
aid consumers to locate particular things in sections and different areas on a website easily,
given that each element in HTML is clearly split up
