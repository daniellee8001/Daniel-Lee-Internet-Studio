Why these movies mean so much to me

Each of these movies made up a massive chunk of my interests growing up, or even the interests I still do have or have come to love now. They are a mixture of my child young self experiencing such joy and wonder from these worlds from these movies that I had come to hold dearly. Many parts of these movies make up my humor, my interests in life, my preference in style of storytelling and medium I like to watch in. It is not the perfect list because a few do shift around here and there, but for some of the older ones, they remain some of the closest aspects of my being. From a child, to a teenager, to a young adult, they will always hold a soft spot in my heart for creating what I am today.

Spirited Away (2001)

A young girl named Chihiro and her parents stumble upon a seemingly abandoned amusement park. After her mother and father are turned into giant pigs, Chihiro meets the mysterious Haku , who explains that the park is a resort for supernatural beings who need a break from their time spent in the earthly realm, and that she must work there to free herself and her parents.

Your Name (2016)

A Silent Voice (2016)

The Lego Movie (2014)

An ordinary lego figurine named Emmet always follows the instructions and rules, is blessed with the path of being an extraoridinary being that will save the world. His adventure goes on as he meets strangers to stop a evil tyrant who will conquer the world.
