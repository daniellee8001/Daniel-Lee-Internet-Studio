Aimed Domain Name

I think the domain name I think will serve my purpose the best is the first option "Oceunity". I expressed my desire for this project to be a place where information could be shared and learned, a place where international borders would be meaningless for the sake of biological research, a sense of unity to be formed, matched well with my theme and of course the final name for the domain. A website very obviously being about the ocean and its far reaching studies of science and biology, alongside the concept of allowing the formation of connection of knowledge among the masses.
