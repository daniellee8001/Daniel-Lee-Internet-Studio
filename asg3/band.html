Twice

Bio

Twice (Korean: 트와이스) is a South Korean girl group made up of nine members.
The names of the members are as follows: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo,
Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu This group was brought up by JYP Entertainment.
In 2016, Twice would rise in domestic popularity with their single "Cheer Up",
this song which charted at number one on the Gaon Digital Chart. This singlehandedly
became the best-performing single of the entire year. Later in June 28, 2017, the
group debuted in Japan under Warner Music Japan following their ablum release called
#Twice. Content taken from Wikipedia

Why this band is my favorite

Twice being my favorite is actually a very new development in my life. It mostly
started due to my peers loving their music, and I would occasionally chime in and listen
with them. I realized over time that their style of music was enticing and a pleasant groove.
A massive development in my favoritism for this group happened with the arrival of the movie
Kpop Demon Hunters. This movie showcased songs both written and performed by some of the Twice
members, more specifically, Jeongyeon, Jihyo, and Chaeyoung. The songs were so massively empowering
and addicting to listen to. The music wouldn't be the calmest of tones, rather a very upbeat and exciting
experience. The songs played for the movie have been stuck in my head for ages. Twice Website
